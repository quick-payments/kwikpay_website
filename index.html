How Kwik Pass Works

1

Get Your Smart Tag

Order your Kwik Pass tag through the app and attach it to your windshield.

2

Approach Payment Point

As you approach a compatible payment point, your tag is automatically detected.

3

Receive Notification

A payment request notification appears on your phone with transaction details.

4

Approve & Go

Tap to approve the payment, and the gate opens automatically. You're all set!

Our Story

About Kwik Pay

Pioneering the future of digital payments in Kuwait.

Our Vision

To lead Kuwait's digital transformation by creating a seamless, cashless ecosystem where payments are instant, secure, and accessible to all segments of society.

Our Mission

To revolutionize everyday transactions through innovative payment solutions that empower consumers, merchants, and corporate clients while maintaining world-class security standards and exceptional user experience.

Our Journey

Quick Payment International Co. is Kuwait's premier financial technology company specializing in comprehensive digital payment solutions. Founded by Mr. Mohammed Alaleem, former Minister of Oil, Electricity & Water in Kuwait, our company was established with a vision to transform the payment landscape in the region.

Starting with a focus on secure mobile transactions, we've rapidly expanded our service portfolio to include advanced payment technologies that serve diverse market segments. Today, we offer a comprehensive suite of solutions including:

  • Mobile wallet services with instant transfers
  • QR code payment systems for merchants
  • Innovative Soundbox technology for payment confirmations
  • Android-based payment terminals for businesses
  • Secure online payment gateways for e-commerce
  • Revolutionary Kwik Pass for contactless vehicle payments

With certifications from the Central Bank of Kuwait and adherence to international security standards, we continue to pioneer financial technology innovations that make everyday transactions simpler, faster, and more secure for all Kuwaitis.
